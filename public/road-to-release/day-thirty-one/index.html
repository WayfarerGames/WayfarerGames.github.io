27 Jun 2023 Polyfury development log

Today I didn’t have a huge amount of time to work on Polyfury, but I managed to get in a few nice little fixes. A 3:30am start means my brain was a little bit mushy today, so nothing happened quickly!

We’re almost a third of the way into this challenge! I’m getting close to getting the fourth boss completed, and I’ve done little bits here and there on the fifth. I also have some code for the final boss written, there are some interesting new mechanics to look forward to there. I think I’m on track for meeting a “final version” with all the bosses done by the middle-ish of August, which is good. In July, I drop half a day from my day job - which means I’ll have all of Wednesday to work on Polyfury with no interruptions 🎉

What’s next?

Tomorrow, this current version gets submitted into certification! That’s happening, if it doesn’t happen feel free to bully me on the Discord server 😉
