5 Jul 2023 Polyfury development log

Today was basically just a huge amount of prep work for the Xbox event this month. I should be able to announce all the details of it tomorrow, and release the new Xbox demo version.

I can imagine most of you reading this are already there, but you should really check out the Discord server (linked at the top of the page) - it’s been nice and active recently, we had a pride logo - congrats to wolf_2174 who won with this awesome design, managing to fit more pride flags in than anyone else 😂

The guys on the server have been really great, and get access to a nice few goodies (including a monthly discord nitro giveaway, and occasionally pizza 🍕). There are also new alpha and beta builds that aren’t available to the public.

What’s next?

Tomorrow is going to be a ton of press outreach, sending all those emails I’ve queued up and finding as many other leads as I can.
