13 Jun 2023 Polyfury development log

Almost 20% through! Today has been a slower day, busy with work and taking a radiator off the wall.

Today I started cooking the Triangle Twins!

This has already proven to be a super fun boss to work on. I’ve been chipping away at it bit by bit over the last few months, I don’t think it’ll take me too long to get enough together to actually put it in front of the alpha testers.

I’m actually really pleased with how each boss actually feels unique - they’re all doing something different, even with the design constraints. Stella has done great work on these, thank you for all your hard work on these designs <3

Oh, and full disclosure - I made another little jam game... I have a problem, ok? 😂

Play it here! https://wayfarergames.itch.io/oh-balls

What’s next?

More triangle stuff, and a little bit more polish for public demos when I get a spare minute.
