## The main difference: Convenience

The Pro version is basically a "batteries included" pack. It comes with a bunch of advanced modules pre-built and tested, so you don't have to write them yourself.

**But here's the secret:** You can build almost everything in the Pro version yourself using the Free version's module API. If you're comfortable with C#, the sky's the limit.

## Which one is right for you?

- **Stick with Free if:** You're a hobbyist, a student, or a programmer who loves writing custom behavior. You want full control and don't mind getting your hands dirty with code.
- **Go Pro if:** You're a professional developer or a team who needs to ship fast. You want complex behaviors (like homing missiles or bouncing lasers) working in seconds, not hours.
